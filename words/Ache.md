# 🧩 常见身体不适英文表达（总结表）

|英文表达|中文意思|常用句型示例|
|---|---|---|
|**headache**|头痛|I have a headache.|
|**stomachache** / **stomach ache**|胃痛 / 肚子痛|I’ve got a stomachache.|
|**back pain** / **backache**|背痛|My back hurts.|
|**toothache**|牙痛|I have a toothache.|
|**sore throat**|喉咙痛|My throat is sore.|
|**fever**|发烧|I think I have a fever.|
|**nausea** / **feel nauseous**|恶心|I feel nauseous.|
|**dizzy** / **dizziness**|头晕|I feel dizzy.|
|**fatigue** / **exhausted**|疲惫、乏力|I feel exhausted.|
|**cough**|咳嗽|I can’t stop coughing.|
|**runny nose**|流鼻涕|I have a runny nose.|
|**shortness of breath**|呼吸困难|I’m experiencing shortness of breath.|
|**chest pain**|胸痛|I have chest pain.|
|**muscle soreness**|肌肉酸痛|My muscles are sore.|
|**joint pain**|关节痛|I have joint pain.|
|**diarrhea**|腹泻|I’ve had diarrhea since yesterday.|
|**constipation**|便秘|I’m constipated.|
|**heartburn**|胃酸逆流、烧心|I have heartburn.|
|**rash**|皮疹|I’ve got a rash on my arm.|
|**earache**|耳朵痛|I have an earache.|
