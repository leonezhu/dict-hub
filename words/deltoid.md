# Deltoid

## Pronunciation
- IPA: `/ˈdɛɫtɔɪd/`
- Phonetic: **deltoid**

## Part of Speech
noun, adjective

## Definitions
1. The deltoid muscle, a triangular muscle on the human shoulder.
2. The deltoid ligament, a triangular ligament on the human ankle.
3. In the shape of the upper case Greek letter delta Δ; triangular.
